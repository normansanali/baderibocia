Sam Faiers diagnosed with Crohn's Disease after mystery six week illness

Publish date: 2024-07-06

    star Sam Faiers has been diagnosed with Crohn's Disease. The 23-year-old reality star underwent a series of tests after suffering with a mystery illness for the past six weeks, and has been told she is suffering with the long-term inflammatory bowel disease.Her spokesperson has confirmed the news, and on Thursday Sam took to Twitter to thank her fans for "all your kind messages".Crohn's Disease can lead to abdominal pain, ulcers and weight loss, and it's thought that petite Sam will now need to dramatically alter her diet — including adhering to a liquid-only diet for six weeks to reset her bowel.
