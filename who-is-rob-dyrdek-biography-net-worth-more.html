Who is Rob Dyrdek: Biography, Net Worth & more, Brief Intro – Robert Stanley Dyrdek mostly referred to as Rob Dyrdek, is an American entrepreneur, producer, actor, reality Tv personality, and former professional skateboarder. He was born to Gene Dyrdek, father, Patty Dyrdek, mother, and Denise Dyrdek, sister.

He is known for dabbling in the entertainment industry hosted the Tv shows; Rob & Big, Rob Dyrdek’s Fantasy Factory, and Ridiculousness. Not just that, he’s also the co-founder of the independent skateboarding company ‘’Alien Workshop and Street League Skateboarding enterprise, in addition to creating an animated Tv series Wild Grinders, the character of Lil Rob.

The entrepreneur is active on most of social media platforms; including Facebook, Twitter, and Instagram as @robdyrdek with 7.4 million followers, his remarkable work is well recognized by his fans.

What is Rob Dyrdek’s profession?

Rob was raised by his parents and alongside his sister in Kettering, Ohio. At an early age, he dropped out of Fairmont high school in his senior year in pursuit of something he was passionate about, skateboarding.

Rob earned his first skateboard from a former professional skateboarder, Neil Blender. His outstanding performance was never left unrecognized as a professional skateboarder. He joined the G&S Skateboarding team and was the youngest member to secure a sponsorship from ‘’ Alien Workshop’’. Dyrdek’s impressive skateboarding skills led him on the road to success, setting more world records than ever.

The professional skateboarder also dabbled in the entertainment industry in 2006 and landed in ‘’Rob & Big’’, and ‘’Big Black’’. He made an appearance in the film ‘’Righteous Kill’’ in 2008 cast in the role of Rambo and others unmentioned. In the business sector, he created several companies including his skate shop and a hip-hop record company, in addition to his own skateboarding league, street league skateboarding.

Whom is Rob Dyrdek married to?
