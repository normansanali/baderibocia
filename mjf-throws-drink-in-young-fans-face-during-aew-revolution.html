MJF Throws Drink In Young Fans Face During AEW Revolution

Publish date: 2024-09-01

Update: MJF genuinely upset the young fan with his heel tactics at AEW Revolution. Sean Sapp reported that the fan got really upset after MJF splattered drink on him. Several people from AEW approached the kid after the incident to cheer him up.

Original: MJF was oozing with confidence as he walked into the ring for his 60-minute Iron Man match against Bryan Danielson in the main event of AEW Revolution tonight. Max didn’t spare the crowd from his heel tactics at the Chase Center.

Click here for our full coverage of AEW Revolution.

MJF entered the crowd during the opening moments of the match. The AEW World Champion trash-talked the San Francisco crowd. He even grabbed a drink from a woman’s hand and threw it in a young fan’s face.

This isn’t the first time MJF has disrespected fans in attendance. Back on the February 15th episode of Wednesday Night Dynamite, MJF once again showed a lack of regard for the fans in attendance by making a controversial remark during a commercial break. Specifically, he dropped brutal line about abortion that left many fans shocked and offended. This is not the first time that MJF has shown such disrespect towards fans, as he has a history of using offensive language and behavior to garner attention.

Despite his controversial behavior, MJF is currently the reigning AEW World Champion and is defending his title against Bryan Danielson in a 60-minute Iron Man match at the ongoing Revolution pay-per-view event. Many fans are eagerly anticipating this highly anticipated showdown and are curious to see if MJF will be able to retain his championship in the face of such a formidable opponent. However, some fans may be hesitant to support MJF given his past behavior and offensive comments. Regardless, the match promises to be a thrilling and intense battle between two of the biggest names in professional wrestling today.
