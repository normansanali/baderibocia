Errol Spence Jr. Height, Weight, Age, Wife, Bio, Family &amp; Facts

Publish date: 2024-09-18

Errol Spence Jr. (born March 03, 1990) is an American Professional Boxer, Public Figure, and Social Media Personality from Long Island, New York. He is best known for being a unified welterweight champion.

As per the source, the boxer held the title of the International Boxing Federation (IBF) title since May 2017. In fact, Errol just bagged the title of the World Boxing Council (WBC) in September 2019.

Furthermore, he grabbed the attention of the public & media after winning the United States national championships three-time continuously. The boxer achieved this during his amateur career in the welterweight division.

In his successful profession, Spence Jr. also performed at the 2012 Olympics and represented his country. However, he was only able to reach the quarter-finals in the competition.

As of October 2019, the sportsman is ranked as the world’s 5th best active boxer by BoxRec and 6th by The Ring magazine. Earlier, in 2015, Spence dubbed as the Prospect of the Year by ESPN.

On October 10, 2019, Errol met with a horrible accident while driving his white Ferrari car and was seriously injured. According to official reports, the boxer even ejected from his Ferrari during a rollover crash in downtown Dallas, Texas.

Tim Smith (Premier Boxing Champions’ spokesman) stated in official reports that Spence Jr.’s injuries are non-life-threatening. He also claimed there is no further information available to the authorities.

Early Life, Childhood, Parents & Profile

The professional boxer, Errol Spence Jr. was born to his mom & dad on the 3rd of March 1990 in Long Island, New York, United States. Hence, his age is 29 years old, as of 2019.

The sportsperson’s father “Errol Spence Sr.” and mother “Debra Spence” rushed to the hospital after they heard about their sons’ accident at 3 a.m. In addition, Spence was in the intensive care unit at Methodist Dallas Medical Center.

His family originally hails from DeSoto, Texas. Errol Sr. worked as a FedEx contractor and even involved in Spence Jr.’s boxing career from the start. On the other hand, his mom is a postal worker.
