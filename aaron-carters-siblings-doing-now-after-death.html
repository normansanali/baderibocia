Aaron Carter's Siblings Are Picking Up The Pieces Of Their Lives After His Death

Highlights

  • Aaron Carter's death was surrounded by speculation.
  • The Carter family has faced multiple heart-wrenching tragedies.

The morning of November 5th, 2022, brought the disheartening headline that Aaron Carter "was discovered unresponsive by his fiancée." Aaron Carter's unexpected death like that of 'Friends' actor, Mathew Perry, sent ripples of shock and sadness far beyond the realm of his fans. He had left behind more than just his legacy of catchy music and a life lived in the spotlight.

Aaron Carter left a family grappling with a profound loss. His siblings are at the forefront, each handling their sadness in their own way. As they try to move forward with their lives, it's not just about finding comfort but also holding onto the memories and the lasting impact Aaron Carter had on their hearts.

How Aaron Carter’s Siblings Are Catching On Since His Tragic Death

Aaron Carter was found dead by his housekeeper in November 2022,and the cause of his death was reported as an accidental drowning which led to his incapacitation in the bathtub. At the time of his death, he was engaged to Melanie Martin, and they had a young son, Princeton Carter, who will grow up hearing stories about his dad.

In the aftermath of the tragic incident, Melanie Martin filed a wrongful death lawsuit on behalf of her son which targets Aaron Carter's doctors and Walgreens pharmacy, alleging that they had improperly provided opioids to him, thus playing a role in his untimely death. Meanwhile, Aaron Carter had always been open about his struggles with addiction, substance abuse and even mental health, for which he reportedly sought treatment for.

Aaron Carter's family also expressed skepticism about the official cause of his death, because they had noticed that he wore a T-shirt and necklace in the bathtub where he had drowned, an act they deemed inconsistent with his usual behavior, asserting that he would never do such.

Even when a family goes through a tough loss, their commitment to remembering their loved ones emphasizes how important it is to work together and support each other during difficult times. Aaron Carter's siblings have actively found meaningful ways to cherish his memory, intending to keep it alive for as long as needed.

Nick Carter, Aaron Carter's brother, was in the process of mending his relationship with Aaron Carter just months before his passing. In an August 2023 interview with ET, Nick shared that the Carter family is still grappling with the aftermath of Aaron's death, expressing profound devastation. He said:

It’s definitely been tough for me and my family, It is still something that we are still processing, to this day. I don’t think we will ever really get over it.

Nick Carter is also actively engaged with On Our Sleeves, a Foundation committed to children's mental health. The Backstreet Boys singer even penned a tribute song titled "Hurts to Love You" to Late Aaron Carter expressing the sorrow he feels in missing him.

Aaron Carter's twin sister, Angel Carter, who shared a close connection with him and holds dear memories, believes that the singer has found peace after his passing. Similar to Nick, Angel Carter is actively engaged with On Our Sleeves foundation and is dedicated to eradicating the stigma around mental health, especially for children. She believes in building a positive legacy from Aaron's tragic death. Angel is married and has a daughter.

Mothers who grieve the loss of their children don't just move on; instead, they learn to carry their sorrows and incorporate the loss into their lives and, in the case of Aaron Carter's mom, Jane Carter, she was profoundly saddened, like any mother would be.

She, however, believes there's more to her son's death than what has been officially stated. Jane strongly feels it could be a case of homicide and thinks the police could have done a better job investigating the circumstances surrounding Aaron Carter's death.

Aaron Carter’s Siblings Have Experienced The Loss Of Other Family Members

The Carter family has faced a series of heart-wrenching tragedies over the years, highlighting the fragility of life and the impacts of fame and personal struggles. The latest tragedy involves the passing of Aaron Carter's older sister, Bobbie Jean Carter, a mother of one. She was discovered dead in the bathroom by emergency workers and subsequently taken to a hospital, where her death was confirmed at the young age of 41, as reported by TMZ.

Bobbie Jean was reportedly on probation for cocaine possession when she passed away in December 2023. Her sudden death was a devastating shock to the Carter family.

This came just months after the death of her brother, Aaron Carter, who died at 34 years. According to County Local News, Bobbie Jean Carter also worked as a makeup and wardrobe stylist for Aaron Carter during his musical career.

The Carter family has faced more than just the losses of Bobbie Jean and Aaron Carter. Another sibling, Leslie Carter, tragically passed away in 2012 at the age of 25 due to complications involving a mix of prescription drugs. Similar to Aaron Carter, Leslie also battled addiction.
