The Death of Garrett Reid

On August 5, 2012, Garrett Reid was found dead in his room at the Philadelphia Eagles’ training camp at Lehigh University. The cause of death was later determined to be an accidental heroin overdose. Garrett’s passing sent shockwaves through the NFL community and brought the Reid family’s private struggles into the public eye.
