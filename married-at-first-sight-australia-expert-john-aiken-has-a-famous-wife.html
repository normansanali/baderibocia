Married at First Sight Australia expert John Aiken has a famous wife

Publish date: 2024-08-06

The expert appears on the E4 show

    Married at First Sight Australia fans adore the show for its bingeable episodes and many dramatic moments – but the three experts are also a solid part of the reality show's brilliance.

    As well as matching contestants together at the beginning, the experts pop up frequently throughout the show to offer their expert advice on marriage, conflicts in relationships, and intimacy to the couples as they navigate the experiment. John Aiken has been a solid staple on the show for a number of seasons – but did you know he has a famous wife? Get to know the star below.

    Who is MAFS expert John Aiken married to?

    expert John is married to Kelly Swanson-Roe who TV fans Down Under may be familiar with thanks to her work as a news reporter based in New Zealand.

    The pair have been together for over 25 years, welcoming two children in that time. John has previously opened up about his wife's strength and resilience after losing her first husband to a canoeing accident. "She is a very resilient woman and quite inspiring to be around, someone who I look up to and who I have a lot of respect for," he told the Australian news outlet, 9Honey.
