Bethenny Frankel Talks Plastic Surgery, Explains Her Shrinking Jaw

Publish date: 2024-07-04

Not on her watch! Bethenny Frankel insisted once again on Wednesday, September 7, that she’s never had plastic surgery.

The Real Housewives of New York City star, 45, was asked if she’s gone under the knife during Watch What Happens Live — and, in her response, explained why her jaw is looking noticeably smaller these days.

“I was wondering if you had any plastic surgery done lately?” a caller asked the Skinnygirl mogul.

“I didn’t,” Frankel said. “Well, I had my jaw injected with Botox because I have TMJ, and if you look at pictures of me from probably, like, seven years ago it’s gradually gotten smaller to the point where I had to stop, and now it actually hurts again.”

Host Andy Cohen interjected: “It does look like your jaw is smaller.”

Back in May, Frankel, who has admitted to grinding her teeth, revealed that her doctor suggested she get Botox in the jawline. The temporomandibular joint (TMJ) connects the jaw to the bones of the skull, and, if irritated, can cause pain. It could be treated with physical therapy, medications or bite guards.

“My face completely changed,” Frankel added of the apparently unintentional transformation.

Frankel went on to acknowledge that she did look a bit different at the recent RHONY reunion. “I looked at the reunion and my eyes — the little trick that actually [makeup artist] Kristofer Buckle taught Maryana, my makeup artist, is when you put your eyelashes on, put the ends up a little bit, and it makes your eyes look a little more out. So they don’t go down,” she explained. “But, no, I have not. I have not had any plastic surgery.”

Back in May, Frankel similarly denied that she’s had plastic surgery during an interview with the Daily Mail. She did, however, admit to getting Botox on other places on her face besides her jaw. “I don’t do it religiously,” she said at the time. “Maybe once a year when I think of it, I’ll be like, ‘Oh, I look like a Shar-Pei, let me do that.”
