Who is Robert Earl Keen?

Robert Earl Keen, born on January 11, 1956, in Houston, Texas, is a renowned American singer-songwriter and a key figure in the Texas country music scene. Known for his storytelling lyrics and a blend of folk, country, and bluegrass styles, Keen has carved out a significant niche in the Americana and alternative country genres. He gained popularity with songs like “The Road Goes on Forever” and “Gringo Honeymoon,” which showcase his unique narrative style and songwriting skills.

Keen’s influence extends beyond his recording career. He’s respected for his contributions to the Texas music scene and his role in shaping the Americana music genre.
