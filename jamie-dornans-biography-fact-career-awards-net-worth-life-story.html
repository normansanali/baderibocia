Jamie Dornan is a Northern Irish musician, model, and actor with an estimated net worth of $3 million. He is globally recognized for playing the titular role of Christian Grey in the phenomenal film adaptation of the worldwide bestselling novel, Fifty Shades of Grey, which was written by E.L. James.

Prior to his successful stint as Christian Grey, Jamie Dornan has appeared in several film and TV shows including Marie Antoinette, Once Upon a Time, and The Fall. As a model, he has represented numerous fashion brands including Abercrombie & Fitch, Hugo Boss, Aquascutum, and Armani. He has also appeared on the major campaigns for Dior Homme and Calvin Klein brands where he got to work with Eva Mendes and Kate Moss.

This celebrity was born as James Dornan on the 1st of May 1982 in Holywood, County Down, Northern Ireland, United Kingdom. After attending Methodist College, he went to Teesside University but later dropped out to pursue a training to become an actor. Since 2013, he has been married to Amelia Warner with whom he has a daughter named Lucie.
