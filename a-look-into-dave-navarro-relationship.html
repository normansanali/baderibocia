    Dave Navarro’s girlfriend

    Dave Navarro was last spotted with his then-girlfriend Andrea Tantaros, a political analyst and Fox pundit. They first met in 2015, when one of their acquaintances introduced them. Navarro, a former Red Hot Chili Peppers guitarist, has many connections.

While dating Tantaros, Navarro was also caught flirting with a mysterious brunette, who many assumed was his new lover.

    Dave Navarro is now married?

    Navarro, host and judge of Ink Master, married Tania Goddard, a famous makeup artist, in 1990. However, Navarro and his first wife called off the wedding after three years. He married his second wife, Rhian Gittins, in 1994, shortly after his first divorce. This love affair lasted for about a month, then the couple requested an annulment.

    Baywatch breakout star Carmen Electra married Navarro in 2003. Fans expected the marriage to last longer than previous marriages, but that didn’t happen. Electra was previously married to Dennis Rodman, a former NBA star, before marrying Navarro.     Navarro and Electra are no longer married but they still keep in touch. On Navarro’s 51st birthday, the Scary movie actress posted a photo of herself and Navarro at her wedding, with the caption, “My love for him is forever.” In 2014, a picture of them together sparked speculation that the two had reunited, although Navarro denied it, saying they were good friends and nothing happened.
