Eddie Kingston vs. Bryan Danielson Set As Blue League Continental Classic Final On 12/27 AEW Dynamit

Publish date: 2024-08-12

The Blue League Continental Classic Final has been set, with Eddie Kingston and Bryan Danielson set to face off next week on AEW Dynamite.

During tonight’s episode of AEW Collision, Kingston defeated Andrade El Idolo, cementing his place as second in the Blue League behind Danielson, who earned the top spot after his draw with Claudio Castagnoli tonight.

Following Kingston’s win tonight, Danielson came down to the ring and the two had a brief staredown, that ended with Danielson and Kingston sharing words with one another.

On the Gold League side of the Continental Classic, it’ll be Swerve Strickland, Jon Moxley, and Jay White going at it, with the two winners of each league meeting at AEW Worlds End to crown the first-ever AEW Continental Classic Champion.
