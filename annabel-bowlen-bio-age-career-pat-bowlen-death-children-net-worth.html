Annabel Bowlen Bio, Age, Career, Pat Bowlen Death, Children, Net Worth

Publish date: 2024-08-15

Annabel Bowlen Biography

Annabel Bowlen is the wife of Denver Broncos owner, Pat Bowlen. Her husband is the majority shareholder in the National Football League team (Denver Broncos) together with his family.

Pat Bowlen, his two brothers, and sister bought the team from Edgar Kaiser in 2014.

Annabel came into limelight after her marriage to millionaire businessman, Pat Bowlen.

Annabel Bowlen Age

Annabel is 66 years old as of 2019. She was born on November 16, 1952, in Edmonton, Canada.

Annabel Bowlen Career

Annabel began her career as a teacher back in Canada. She taught grade school students in her early days.

She later became a figure skating trainer – a hobby for which she became popular in the country. She has also worked as an attorney in Edmonton.

Annabel Bowlen Husband

Annabel was married to millionaire Pat Bowlen until his death on June 13, 2019. Annabel and Pat met in Edmonton, Canada.

At that time, Annabel was working as an attorney. Both of them later moved into the United States where Pat took over duties as a major owner and Chief Executive Officer of the Denver Broncos. It is still not clear when they got married.

Pat Bowlen Death

Pat Bowlen died on June 13, 2019. His family confirmed in a statement on June 14, 2019 saying;

“We are saddened to inform everyone that our beloved husband and father, Pat Bowlen, passed on to the next chapter of his life late Thursday night peacefully at home surrounded by family. His soul will live on through the Broncos, the city of Denver and all of our fans.”

Pat had been battling Alzheimer’s disease. In a statement that was posted on the Denver Broncos’ website, the family paid tribute to their patriarch and thanked the fans.

“Our family wishes to express its sincere gratitude for the outpouring of support we have received in recent years. Heaven got a little bit more orange and blue tonight.”

In June 2018, Annabel announced she also had been diagnosed with Alzheimer’s.

Pat died at the age of 75.

Annabel Bowlen Children

Annabel and Pat Bowlen had five children together. Pat also had two more from his previous marriage with Sally Parker.

With Pat, Annabel had John, Patrick, Brittany, Annabel, and Christianna.

Annabel Bowlen Net Worth

Annabel and her late husband Pat have an estimated net worth of $1 Billion.
