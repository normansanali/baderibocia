Kourtney Kardashian Wears Two Gothic Pre-Wedding Looks in Italy

Publish date: 2024-08-19

Kourtney Kardashian has brought her edgy bridal aesthetic to the Italian Riviera.

The Poosh founder is throwing her third wedding with Blink-182 drummer Travis Barker in Portofino this weekend, and the couple has already attended several pre-ceremony outings with friends and family. The reality star's looks so far, all by Dolce & Gabbana, are going all in on the gothic style that was teased with the bridal minidress she wore to her intimate courthouse ceremony.

For a Friday night dinner at Ristorante Puny, the star swore a sultry red look by the Italian luxury house, featuring a strapless mesh maxi dress with floral decorations layered over a corset bodysuit. She finished the look with strappy red sandals and a maroon fur wrap drapped over her shoulders.

NINO//Getty Images

The bride was also spotted in a dark iteration of her wedding minidress during a Saturday outing, where she held hands with Barker and her son Reign as they made their way to a pre-wedding lunch. The gothic look included a figure-hugging black minidress with an image of the Virgin Mary dressed in blue on the front, matching black opera gloves, strappy heeled sandals and a short black veil with a blue lace trim.

The musician coordinated in his own all-black look, pairing a full-length robe with fringe detailing with black boots, a silver chainlink necklace, and a pair of black sunglasses.

NINO//Getty Images

Kardashian and Barker will reportedly marry at the Castello Brown, a 16th-century castle in the seaside Italian town. The couple officially tied the knot in a courthouse ceremony in Santa Barbara, CA, earlier this week, just months after their "practice wedding" at a Las Vegas chapel. The reality star shared pics from the surprise ceremony on Instagram, including snaps of herself wearing the bridal Dolce & Gabbana minidress while posing in a vintage car with a "Just Married" sign on the back.

"They had to legally get married first ahead of their big Italian wedding, which is happening very soon," an insider told People. "All the details are set and the whole family, including all the kids, are very excited."
