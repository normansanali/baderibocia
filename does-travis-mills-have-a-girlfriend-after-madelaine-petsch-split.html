Travis Mills is the co-host of MTV’s Help! I’m in a Secret Relationship where he explored why individuals in long-term romances were kept away from their partner’s family and friends. But what about Travis’ love life? Does he have a girlfriend after his split with Madelaine Petsch? Keep reading to find out. 

Does Travis Mills Have A Girlfriend After Madelaine Petsch Split?

No, as of June 2022, Travis Mills didn’t have a girlfriend after his split with Madelaine Petsch.

His IG feeds, then, were riddled with selfies, and offered no hints, whatsoever, as to who the host might be dating at the moment.

Guess, the only “girlfriend” Travis, then had, was his band called “Girlfriends” — which Travis formed with his buddy/professional drummer Nick Gross.

Travis Mills And Madelaine Petsch’s Relationship

Travis Mills and Madelaine Petsch officially began their relationship in Feb 2017.

To recall, it was Travis who first snuck into Madelaine’s Facebook DMs in January 2017, to congratulate her on landing a role on Jughead. At the time, the duo had had mutual friends, so they just added each other but rarely kept in touch.

The next month, Travis then made his first move and asked Madelaine out to a movie. They hung out and spent every single day together thereafter.

And by “thereafter” we mean until February 2020. Exactly 3 years later, the pair then announced their split, with Travis also shedding light on their relationship through a long IG post.

In the caption, Travis described that though they seem like “one-dimensional people, free of real-world challenges, kicking our feet up, waiting for the next big,” they were just “ambitious and hard-working individuals, dealing with rejection, in different states/countries, wondering what the next best move is, trying to see friends/family, take care of our dogs, answering an ocean of e-mails, balancing REAL LIFE doing it all while being separated by it.⁣⁣⁣⁣”

There was a mountain of challenges, they tried to cross together! Unfortunately, it took a toll on them, so much that they decided to go their separate ways. However, if he had a chance to go back he’d happily do it all over again alongside this “compassionate, smart, and wonderful,” soul.

Also, Travis promised that he wouldn’t be deleting any of their photographs over his socials. “I regret nothing.⁣⁣,” he remarked.

Madelaine Petsch Relationship Status Today

Madelaine Petsch could be dating Miles Chamley-Watson today, as of June 2022. The two were spotted walking hand in hand in Vancouver, Canada, the last we checked.

Also, the rumored couple was first spotted together in March 2021. Means, it’s been over a year since something’s been cooking between the two.

Miles was Madelaine first rumored boyfriend after he split with Travis. It seems that though Travis took the break up well, Madelaine’s “mental health was at an all-time low” at the time. Plus, given the COVID-19 quarantine, “Every day was definitely a challenge and therapy was absolutely key for me to keep sane,” Madelaine explained.
