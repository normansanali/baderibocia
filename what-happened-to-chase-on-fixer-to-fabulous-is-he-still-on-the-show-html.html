What happened to Chase on Fixer to Fabulous, is he still on the show?

Publish date: 2024-09-28

Fixer to Fabulous, the HGTV sensation, brings forth the enchanting world of home transformations. Jenny and Dave Marrs, the dynamic duo, work their magic on homes in Bentonville, Arkansas. The show, now in its third season, continues to captivate viewers with awe-inspiring renovations.

Meet Chase Looney: The Carpenter Extraordinaire

Chase Looney, renowned as a carpenter and construction manager, is a pivotal figure on Fixer to Fabulous. His journey began at the young age of 14 when he embarked on carpentry training. By 20, he had achieved mastery in the craft. Later, in 2009, Chase joined the Bentonville Fire Department.

Triumph Over Adversity

In a heartbreaking turn of events, Chase faced a life-altering accident, losing an eye just days after filming the show’s pilot episode. A firework accident during a Fourth of July gathering led to this tragic incident. Despite this adversity, Chase’s determination and spirit continue to inspire.

Chase’s Unwavering Presence

As of 2021, Chase remains an integral part of Fixer to Fabulous. While he may not feature in every single episode, his absence is deeply felt by fans. His invaluable contributions to the show solidify his position as an irreplaceable member of Jenny and Dave’s team.
