One Direction: Which Boy Band Member Has the Best Abs, Sings in Shower

Publish date: 2024-09-08

The boys of One Direction continue to share their lives with their millions of fans worldwide! The five faces of the popular British boy band — Niall Horan, Zayn Malik, Liam Payne, Harry Styles, Louis Tomlinson — cover the December/January issue of Seventeen magazine on six separate covers, with each bandmate posing individually and sharing one dreamy group cover shot.

Fronting the teen mag's Party Issue, the boy banders open up about their bromance: "I’ve been labeled the prankster," Louis Tomlinson, 21, told Seventeen when asked about how the guys have fun on the road. "I change the words of songs when the guys least expect it — their reactions are priceless,” the soccer-playing singer added.

Harry Styles, 19, also chirped that every member of the quintet sings in the shower: "We all do! We can sometimes hear Liam [Payne] through the wall," Taylor Swift's ex shared with the magazine.
