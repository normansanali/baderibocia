The Repair Shops Angelina Bakalarou is a conservator and all-around creative

Publish date: 2024-07-07
Spread the love             

“The Repair Shop,” a beloved BBC series, welcomes skilled artisans who master the art of restoring various items. Among the talented experts, Angelina Bakalarou has earned admiration as the show’s proficient paper and paintings conservator. Her work on the series has left an indelible mark, impressing fans with her remarkable skills. In this article, we explore the journey of Angelina Bakalarou, her role on “The Repair Shop,” and her creative expressions shared on Instagram.

Meet Angelina Bakalarou

Angelina Bakalarou boasts an impressive career as a paper and paintings conservator. According to her LinkedIn profile, she has dedicated over five years to The Conservators, honing her expertise. Her professional journey also includes a remarkable stint of nearly nine years at The National, where she served as a project conservator and later assumed the role of conservation manager. Her passion for conservation shines through her academic pursuits. Angelina completed her Bachelor’s degree in Conservation of Antiquities and Works of Art at the Technological Educational Institute of Athens from 2005 to 201She furthered her knowledge by achieving a Master’s degree in Conservation of Art on Paper.

Angelina on “The Repair Shop”

In January 2023, Angelina Bakalarou became a valued addition to BBC’s “The Repair Shop.” This heartwarming series features experts who specialize in various restoration areas. One such specialist, Julyan Wallis, is a luthier who skillfully repairs stringed instruments like guitars and violins. At the onset of her journey on “The Repair Shop,” Angelina received a warm welcome from co-star Will Kirk. As Will shared on Instagram, Angelina is not just a colleague but also his friend. He introduced her as the show’s new paper conservator, emphasizing the camaraderie that exists among the team, including Jay Blades.

Angelina’s Creative Expressions on Instagram

Beyond her incredible work on “The Repair Shop,” Angelina Bakalarou’s Instagram reveals a multifaceted talent. Her profile, @angelina_bak, offers a glimpse into her world of creativity. While she expertly restores paper items, Angelina also showcases her artistic abilities through drawings. Her Instagram is a canvas of vibrant flowers, picturesque landscapes, and captivating sketches. Angelina generously shares her artwork with her followers, enriching her profile with artistic diversity.

Judging by her Instagram, Angelina appears to embrace a love for art and appears to have a zest for life. Her posts range from showcasing her art pieces to endearing glimpses of feline companions. Her creative expressions add an extra layer to her multifaceted personality.

Conclusion

Angelina Bakalarou, the talented paper conservator of “The Repair Shop,” has skillfully revived cherished items while leaving a lasting impression on fans. Her journey from an academic pursuit in art conservation to becoming a revered expert on the series showcases her dedication. Beyond her professional role, Angelina’s creative expressions on Instagram reveal the colorful dimensions of her personality. As a valued member of “The Repair Shop” team, she continues to capture the hearts of viewers with her remarkable skills and artistic talents.
