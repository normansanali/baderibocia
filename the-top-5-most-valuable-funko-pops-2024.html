The Top 5 Most Valuable Funko Pops 2024

Publish date: 2024-08-19

Funko Pops have become an increasingly popular collectible over the years, with some of them becoming highly valuable due to their rarity and demand.

In 2024, the world of Funko Pops has seen some remarkable additions and releases, leading to the creation of some of the most sought-after and valuable pieces for collectors.

Courtesy of Fan Arch, here are the top 5 most valuable Funko Pops in 2024.

1. Mythic Pack Digital Pop! Vinyl Figure

The Mythic Pack Digital Pop! Vinyl Figure is one of the most valuable Funko Pops in 2024. This highly limited edition Pop stands out not only for its rarity but also for its size, shape, and quantity.

The Mythic Pack contains 30 Digital Pop! figures and guarantees the inclusion of a Digital Pop! with a Mythic rarity.

These Mythic Digital Pop! figures are eligible to redeem for the Mythic vinyl figure, which is the rarest of the rare with only 499 Pop! vinyl figures available.

The first drop of the Mythic Pack will be featured in the Funko Funime and Cosplay drop on May 14, 2024, at 11 am PT on Droppp. The Mythic Pop! vinyl figure included in this pack will be a highly limited 10” tall figure, designed to be a standout addition to any collection.

With such exclusivity and rarity, the Mythic Pack Digital Pop! Vinyl Figure is undoubtedly one of the most valuable Funko Pops in 2024.

2. Grail Digital Pop! Vinyl Figure

The Grail Digital Pop! Vinyl Figure is another highly valuable Funko Pop in 2024.

Similar to the Mythic Pack, the Grail Digital Pop! is known for its rarity and exclusivity. These figures are often highly sought after by collectors due to their limited availability.

The Grail Digital Pop! figures are celebrated for their unique designs and themes, making them a prized possession for any Funko Pop enthusiast.

3. Legendary Digital Pop! Vinyl Figure

The Legendary Digital Pop! Vinyl Figure holds a significant position among the most valuable Funko Pops in 2024. Just like the Grail and Mythic Pops, the Legendary Digital Pop! is revered for its rarity and desirability.

4. 10” Limited Edition Pop! Vinyl Figures

In 2024, 10” Limited Edition Pop! Vinyl Figures continue to hold substantial value within the Funko Pop collectibles market. These larger-sized figures are often released in limited quantities, making them highly sought after by collectors. 

5. Exclusive Convention and Retailer Pops

Exclusive Convention and Retailer Pops retain their value as some of the most sought-after Funko Pops in 2024. These exclusive releases are often limited in quantity and feature special designs or characters that are not available in regular retail stores. 

 

The world of Funko Pops in 2024 continues to see the release of highly valuable and sought-after collectibles.

From limited edition packs to exclusive convention and retailer Pops, the market is filled with valuable pieces that are highly coveted by collectors.

As the demand for rare and exclusive Funko Pops continues to rise, the value of these collectibles is expected to remain high, making them an attractive investment for enthusiasts and collectors alike.
