Celina Powell Posts Alleged Explicit Video With Lil Meech On OnlyFans Amid Summer Walker Romance

Publish date: 2024-09-22

In the time since Summer Walker and Lil Meech's not-so-lowkey romance has been outed to the world, they've mostly gotten positive feedback. The R&B songstress is best known for her tumultuous relationship with London On Da Track. Though traumatizing, it ultimately inspired her Still Over It album. After healing from that, she spent some time dating Larry. They now share twins, but ultimately decided to call it quits before Walker gave birth.

In the public eye, the Atlanta native was focusing on herself and her music for most of 2023. Her Clear 2: Soft Life EP is set to drop later this month. She's also already given stellar performances at both Dreamville Festival and Something in the Water. Now, however, she seems to have fallen hard for the BMF actor. Walker has been making regular appearances on his Instagram Story over the past few days. She certainly looks happy, as many fans have noticed. Unfortunately, Celina Powell is seemingly doing what she can to stir up drama between the apparent new couple.

Celina Powell Exposes Lil Meech, Trolls Summer Walker

Meech first posted a photo of the "No Love" hitmaker wearing his signature chain. Afterward, Powell's own Story lit up with a selfie of her in the same necklace. To add insult to injury, she soundtracked the mirror selfie with Walker's "I'll Kill You." As if that weren't enough to light the mother of three's fire, the salacious socialite also promoted a link to her OnlyFans page. She's apparently uploaded an explicit video of her and the 23-year-old actor getting intimate.

As VladTV notes, Powell has previously been linked to the likes of DJ Akademiks, Snoop Dogg, and Offset, and has become known for bringing drama into the lives of those who get involved with her. Even with all the internet drama, Summer Walker appears to be unbothered, still capturing smiley photos of herself to share on her beau's page.
