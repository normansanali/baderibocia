The Top 10 Warriors Players of All Time

Publish date: 2024-08-06

The Golden State Warriors, one of the most successful franchises in NBA history, have had a plethora of talented players throughout their rich history. From the early days in Philadelphia to their dominant reign in Oakland, the Warriors have seen some exceptional talent grace their roster. In this article, we will delve into the top 10 Warriors players of all time, who have made a lasting impact on the team and the league as a whole.

1. Stephen Curry

There is no doubt that Stephen Curry tops the list of the greatest Warriors players of all time. With his unmatched shooting ability and incredible ball-handling skills, Curry has revolutionized the game of basketball. His ability to shoot from anywhere on the court and his exceptional dribbling skills have made him a nightmare for opposing defenses. Curry's three-point shooting prowess has earned him two MVP awards and three NBA championships with the Warriors.

2. Wilt Chamberlain

Wilt Chamberlain, one of the most dominant players in NBA history, was a force to be reckoned with during his time with the Warriors. Chamberlain's sheer size and athleticism allowed him to dominate the game like no other player before him. He averaged an astonishing 50.4 points per game during the 1961-62 season, a record that still stands today. Chamberlain's impact on the Warriors franchise cannot be overstated.

3. Rick Barry

Rick Barry, known for his unorthodox underhand free throw shooting style, was a key player for the Warriors during the 1970s. Barry's scoring ability, combined with his exceptional basketball IQ, made him a formidable opponent for any team. He led the Warriors to their first and only NBA championship in 1975 and was named NBA Finals MVP. Barry's impact on the franchise and his contributions to the game of basketball make him a deserving member of this list.

4. Kevin Durant

Although Kevin Durant only spent three seasons with the Warriors, his impact on the team was undeniable. Durant's scoring ability and versatility made him a perfect fit for the Warriors' fast-paced, high-scoring style of play. During his tenure with the team, Durant won two NBA championships and was named NBA Finals MVP both times. His decision to join the Warriors in 2016 sent shockwaves throughout the league and solidified the team as a powerhouse.

5. Chris Mullin

Chris Mullin, known for his smooth shooting stroke and offensive prowess, spent the majority of his career with the Warriors. Mullin was a key player during the "Run TMC" era, alongside Tim Hardaway and Mitch Richmond. He was a five-time NBA All-Star and played an essential role in revitalizing the franchise during the 1980s and 1990s. Mullin's contributions to the Warriors' success cannot be overlooked.

6. Nate Thurmond

Nate Thurmond, a dominant force in the paint, played 11 seasons with the Warriors and was one of the greatest centers of his era. His shot-blocking ability and rebounding prowess made him a formidable opponent for any team. Thurmond's impact on the Warriors' success during his time with the team cannot be overstated.

7. Klay Thompson

Klay Thompson, known for his incredible shooting ability and lockdown defense, has been an integral part of the Warriors' success in recent years. Thompson's ability to catch fire and score points in bunches has made him one of the most dangerous shooters in NBA history. His partnership with Stephen Curry in the Warriors' backcourt has been instrumental in the team's championship runs.

8. Draymond Green

Draymond Green, known for his versatility and defensive prowess, has been the heart and soul of the Warriors' success in recent years. Green's ability to guard multiple positions and his high basketball IQ have made him an invaluable asset to the team. His leadership on and off the court has been instrumental in the Warriors' championship runs.

9. Paul Arizin

Paul Arizin, one of the greatest scorers of his era, spent his entire career with the Warriors. Arizin's scoring ability and offensive versatility made him a standout player during his time. He was a 10-time NBA All-Star and led the Warriors to their first NBA Finals appearance in 1956. Arizin's impact on the franchise cannot be overlooked.

10. Bernard King

Bernard King, a prolific scorer during his time with the Warriors, was known for his ability to put the ball in the basket. King's scoring ability and offensive skills made him a fan favorite during his tenure with the team. Although his time with the Warriors was relatively short, King's impact on the franchise was significant.

The Golden State Warriors have seen some outstanding players throughout their history. From the sharpshooting of Stephen Curry to the dominance of Wilt Chamberlain, these players have left a lasting impact on the team and the league. While this list captures the top 10 players of all time, it is important to acknowledge that there have been many other great players who have represented the Warriors with distinction.
