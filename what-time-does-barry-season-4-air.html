What time does Barry season 4 air?

Publish date: 2024-09-23

The time has finally come for Bill Hader’s misadventures as Barry Berkman to come to an end in tonight’s series finale of HBO’s Barry. From when we last saw Barry in the season three finale, his misdeeds had finally caught up to him after being apprehended and arrested by a S.W.A.T team in an elaborate setup by Jim. However, season 4 has seen him escape prison and the show has had a huge time skip – and now his family is in grave danger.

We’re a matter of hours away from discovering Barry’s ultimate fate. Is he going to live? Is he going to die? Will Sally and Barry’s son learn the truth of his “reformed Christian” father? We’ll be getting our answers very shortly, but what time is it all going down? We’ve got all of the answers for you here. 

What time is the Barry finale releasing?  

While season four of Barry has usually been airing on HBO and Max at 10:00 p.m. ET / 9:00 p.m. CT / 7:00 p.m. PT, unfortunately we’ll need to wait just a little longer for the finale, because it just to happens that Succession is also airing its finale right beforehand, and it will be clocking in at 90 minutes. Assuming you’re not getting in on the action of the hit boardroom drama, then the time to fire up the television for Barry tonight is 10:30 p.m. ET / 9:30 p.m. CT / 7:30 p.m. PT.

All eight episodes of the fourth season have been directed by series star Bill Hader himself, and quite frankly, we’re itching with anticipation to see what ends up happening to Barry and all of the fan-favorite characters around him.
