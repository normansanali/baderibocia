Luke Nichols - Trivia, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-08-05

Professional angler and fishing sports journalist for Carppro magazine. He updates his Catfish and Carp YouTube channel with twice-weekly content during fishing seasons. He has over 1 million subscribers and 281 million total views. His content features tips on catching large lake fish. 

Before Fame

He earned a degree in law. 

Trivia

After taking trips to the UK to study how British and American fishing techniques differed, he blended both into a style for catching lake fish. He demonstrates his knowledge in his videos.

Family Life

He has lived in a cabin near the Occoquan reservoir so he could have constant access to its catfish and carps. He is married to his wife Rebecca. They have three children. He learned to fish as a boy watching his older brother, father, and grandfather. 

Associated With

He is similar to YouTube fishing star Michael Hsiao with their emphasis on video tutorials and catching large fish.
