How do I update my instagram app?

How to Update Your Instagram App
  • First of all, go to your mobile device’s Settings.
  • Scroll down to the “iTunes and App Store” section.
  • There will be a section for automatic downloads, tap to toggle the “Updates” option to complete the process.
  • Voila! Automatic updates are now available for your device.

    • How do I update my Instagram to the latest version?

    First, go to the App Store, located at the home screen. Next, tap the updates tab located at the bottom right side of the screen. Depending on how many updates are available, there will be a red dot with a number showing how many updates there are. You’ll be taken to a list of apps that can be updated.

    Why won’t my Instagram update to the new version?

    It’s normal for big apps to slowly release new features. The new feature might not be available in your country yet or it might not be on your account yet. It’s also possible that one of your accounts already has the new feature while another one of your accounts might not have it yet.
