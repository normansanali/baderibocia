Baron Hotel Heliopolis, Cairo, Egypt

Publish date: 2024-08-10
The Baron Hotel Heliopolis, Cairo is ideally located in the heart of Heliopolis area, an upscale residential, business and leisure district that hosts Cairo International Airport and has easy access to some of the most popular commercial and industrial zones in Cairo and on its outskirts such as the 10th of Ramadan and El Obour Cities.
Baron Hotel Heliopolis, Cairo, a boutique-style corporate Hotel, overlooks the famous historical Baron Empain Palace built in 1911 and is situated three km from the largest retail and leisure complex in Cairo, five km from Cairo International Airport and Cairo International Conference Center (CICC), and 10 km from down-town and the Cairo Opera House.
Established in 1980, Baron Hotel Heliopolis, Cairo is the first foundation for Baron Hotels & Resorts Egypt.
Being an ideal starting point for sightseeing and enjoying the vibrant life of Cairo, the “Business Hotel with a History” is an ideal retreat for business and leisure seekers.
