Benji Madden Hollywood Life

Publish date: 2024-08-30

Bio:
Benji Madden (born Benjamin Levi Combs in Waldorf, Maryland on March 11, 1979) is a guitarist and singer for the band Good Charlotte. He is in the band with his identical twin brother, Joel Madden. Good Charlotte released it’s first full-length self-titled studio album in Sept. 2000. The band has since released 4 more studio albums, and 2 compilations. Throughout his music career, Benji also made cameo appearances in the films Material Girl and Not Another Teen Movie. He also owns his own clothing line, DCMA Collective.

Best Known For:
Benji is best known for forming Good Charlotte with Joel.

Personal Life:
Benji has two other siblings, an older brother Josh, and a younger sister, Sarah. In May 2014, Benji began dating actress Cameron Diaz. Only seven months later it was confirmed that they were engaged, and the couple later married in Jan. 2015. Prior to marrying Cameron, Benji was formerly engaged to Australian actress Sophie Monk, briefly dated Paris Hilton, and has been romantically linked to Holly Madison and Tila Tequila.
