OMG! Jennifer Aniston Copies Brenda and Kelly's Spring Dance Dress

Publish date: 2024-07-20

9021-whoa!

The black-and-white Valentino frock Jennifer Aniston wore to the 26th American Cinematheque Award Gala Nov. 15 had ’90s teen soap fans doing a double-take. The reason? Its resemblance to one of the most memorable TV dresses of all time — the off-the-shoulder gown worn by both Kelly (Jennie Garth) and Brenda (Shannen Doherty) to their Spring Dance on Beverly Hills, 90210 back in 1993.

Aniston’s dress is a little more understated than the coordinating ensembles worn by Kelly and Brenda, whose evening out took a turn for the scandalous when she lost her virginity to Dylan (Luke Perry). The We’re the Millers star’s night, by comparison, was fairly tame: She and fiance Justin Theroux swung by the event (which took place in Beverly Hills!) to pay homage to pal Ben Stiller.

Instead, “Everyone was talking about her [engagement] ring!” an attendee tells Us Weekly.
