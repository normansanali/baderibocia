Meet Jamie Sayed, star of Peacock’s brand new TV show Below Deck: Down Under, from the Below Deck franchise. Since the premiere, he has earned attention with his good looks but there is more to learn about him.

Meet Jamie Sayed, Bosun On Below Deck: Down Under

Peacock network is offering viewers the new spin-off of Below Deck titled Down Under. The new show is based in Australia this time and it premiered on 18 March 2022. And like always, viewers are excited to learn about the crew line-up who are set to sail around Whitsunday Islands and the Great Barrier Reef.

Jamie Sayed has joined the show as Buson in the superyacht M/Y Thalassa. Joining him on the cruise are Captain Jason Chambers, chief stew Aesha Scott, chef Ryan McKeown, second stew Tumi Mhlongo, third stew Magda Ziomek, deckhand Culver Bradbury, female deckhand Brittni Burton, and Ben Crawley, the final member of the deck team.

According to Distractify, the show might as well air on BravoTV.

In mid-February 2022, Jamie announced on his Instagram, “Well I guess I’m no longer in stealth mode… 3,2,1 let’s go… Catch me as your Bosun on Below Deck Down Under for a world of fun, sun, yachts, amazing beaches, and a bunch of drama!!
Are you ready?? Watch it from March 17th Streaming only on @peacocktv And on @hayuau for my Aussie friends and family.”

On 7 March, he again updated his Instagram followers, “Wow it’s getting real now 10 days to go until the premiere of #BelowDeckDownUnder !!”.

How Much Is Jamie Sayed Net Worth?

As of 2022, Jamie Sayed has been able to successfully accumulate a net worth under $350K.

Starting from 2017, Jamie has been working in the yachting industry for over 4 years. He joined Below Deck as bosun, an in-charge of equipment and the crew. According to the report of Slice, Bosun makes approximately $5,000 a month and $52,000 a season.

Besides his job, Jamie also hosts a podcast named Wake Up With Jamie. The show is “all about everyday people and their stories. We all have a story and Jamie will interview a range of different people and ask them to share their stories on how they got to where they are now and where they came from. These stories will be motivational, happy, maybe a little sad at times, inspirational and the listeners (you) maybe be able to relate to some of these stories.”

In Episode 31 of the podcast, Jamie opened up about the show and residing in West Palm Beach, Florida. Furthermore, he also has a travel vlog going by the same name Wake Up With Jamie.
