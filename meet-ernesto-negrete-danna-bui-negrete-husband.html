Meet Ernesto Negrete, RHOSLC Star Danna Bui-Negrete Husband

In the show, Danna didn’t hold back when it came to what she thought of Jen. And this meant she jeopardized her friendship with Heather when she had to bring up a rumor about her close pal, Jen.

Besides this, she was also successfully running a cafe called Spill The Tea.

This reality star turned 34 on October 22, 2021. Wishing her a happy birthday, Ernesto took it to his FB to share, “Happy Birthday my love! 34 never looked so good. I’m so proud of the woman, mother, and wife you have become! You’re the best!”
