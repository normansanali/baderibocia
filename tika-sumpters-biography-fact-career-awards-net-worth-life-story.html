American model, singer, and actress Tika Sumpter has an estimated net worth of $1.5 million. She is best known for her recurring role on Gossip Girl as Raina Thorpe. She is also noted for her appearance on The Game, a sitcom on The CW/BET, where she played the role of Jenna Rice. Starting in 2013, she has been playing the role of Candace Young on The Haves and the Have Nots.

Sumpter was born Euphemia Sumpter on June 20, 1980 in Queens, New York, USA. She attended Marymount Manhattan College, where she took a degree majoring in Communications.
