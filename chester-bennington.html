Chester Bennington popularly known as Chester Charles Bennington was an American singer, songwriter, actor, musician and producer. As of 2017, Chester Bennington’s net worth is $30 million. Bennington originally earned popularity as a singer with the international commercial success of Linkin Park’s first album.

The Recording Industry Association of America certified the album Diamond in 2005, making it the best-selling debut album of the decade and one of the few to do so. From Meteora (2003) through One More Light (2017), Linkin Park’s subsequent studio albums maintained the band’s popularity.
