Si Robertson Biography and Wiki

Si Robertson (born as Silas Merritt Robertson) also known as “Uncle Si”, is an American television personality, preacher, hunter, and outdoorsman. He is also a retired U.S. Army soldier and a retired duck-call maker at Duck Commander. Robertson is well known for his role on A&E’s Duck Dynasty, on which he has emerged as the breakout character.

Si Robertson Education

Robertson was enrolled at Caddo High School. He then idolized his elder brother Phil during this time and this led him to follow in his athletic footsteps, playing football for his high school’s team. After his graduation from Caddo High, Robertson got enrolled at Louisiana Tech University in Ruston, Louisiana. However, he would only stay there for three quarters. During the Vietnam War and he got drafted into the U.S. Army and had to drop out of school. Robertson made it to the rank of Sergeant First Class (E-7) before retiring in 1993. Si even has an exhibit in his honor on display at the Chennault Aviation and Military Museum located in Monroe, in his home state of Louisiana.

Si Robertson Army

Robertson was drafted into the United States Army during the Vietnam War. During the war, he was sent a set of Tupperware cups, beans and rice, two boots, and two jars of jalapeño peppers in each boot because he loves jalapeño peppers by his mother.  He later retired from the Army in 1993 with the rank of Sergeant First Class (E-7). He got recognized for his military career with an exhibit at the Chennault Aviation and Military Museum in Monroe, Louisiana. After having retired from the Army in 1993, he found employment at his brother Phil’s Duck Commander business. Robertson has been a big part of the growing business.

Si Robertson Career

While Robertson was still serving in the army, his brother Phil started a company that was in the business of making duck calls. When Robertson came back home from the army in 1993, his brother brought him on board that helped him grow the business. His job was to make the reeds which were a component of the duck calls. He has often been featured in Duck Dynasty as being lax in his responsibilities for the business.

Robertson is undoubtedly the star of the show and this is mostly due to his excellently humorous disposition and spontaneity. In the series finale of the Duck Dynasty show, Robertson got to be that he intended to start a band. He as well started at the band after the show came to an end in March 2017 and called it Uncle Si and The Sicotics. He has also been featured in a number of television shows including Good Morning America. His reality series portfolio also includes Duck Commander, The Duckmen of Lousiana, and his self-titled show Going Si-Ral. Si has also done some voicing/narration for the children’s animated series VeggieTales.

Si and his entire family are widely known for their Christian faith; the man takes every chance he gets to express his religious beliefs. Whenever Robertson signs an autograph, he always writes down his favorite bible verse John 3:16 and 17. According to the ex-soldier, it is a way of reminding people that God loves everyone and that no one is ever beyond redemption.

Si Robertson Book
