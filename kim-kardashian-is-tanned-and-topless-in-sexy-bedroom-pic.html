Kim Kardashian Is Tanned and Topless in Sexy Bedroom Pic

Publish date: 2024-09-01

Wake up, Mrs. West! Kim Kardashian is showing off her toned physique in a new Instagram photo that leaves little to the imagination.

The Keeping Up With the Kardashians star shared a topless photo of herself on Friday, January 5, writing: “Rise & Grind.” In the snap, she is laying in an all-white bed, wearing nothing but a white thong. The reality star is covering her bare chest with her hands.

The risqué photo was taken by photographer Eli Russell Linnetz‏, who was the one behind the Kardashian family Christmas card photos that kept fans glued to her feed for 25 days this holiday season.

After Christmas, the Selfish author deleted all of the photos saying that “was always the plan.” After fans noticed the pics were missing and asked about them, she tweeted on December 28: “I archived them so my Instagram feed vibe is back.”

The mom of two, who is currently expecting her third child via surrogate, has been known to show off her curves on social media. She has been open about her new workout routine and lifestyle change that helped her achieve a new fitness level after giving birth to her son Saint, 2.

The 37-year-old told Allure in September that she is now “the most fit I’ve ever been” after hiring a female bodybuilder she came across on Instagram who also “had an amazing transformation after she had a baby.”

“She has moved a lot more to strength training,” a source told Us Weekly that same month. “It’s about toning.” The KKW Beauty founder has also changed eating habits, and now eats small meals throughout the day, including healthy snacks and avoids late-night eating.
